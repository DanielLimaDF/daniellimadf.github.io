EatSpecific

EatSpecific is an App designed to help people with specific dietary needs. AppStore was missing an app to helps these people to find places of good quality food that meets their needs, such as: Lactose intolerance, Gluten intolerance, allergy to certain foods, health problems that interfere with eating habits, etc.

Features

  • Display of restaurants based on the specific diets that the dishes of the menu serve
  • Search for dishes and restaurant based on dietetic specification
  • Search for dishes and restaurants based on tags provided to classified them
  • Register food preferences, allergies, intolerances or specific diets to see alerts recommending or not the dishes in the menu
  • Share restaurants or dishes
  • Save favorite dishes and restaurants
  • Evaluate restaurants and dishes
  • Call the restaurant
  • Navigate to the destination (restaurant) using Waze or Apple Maps
